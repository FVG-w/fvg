Privacy Policy for Film Vault Gazette

Introduction:
Welcome to Film Vault Gazette. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you visit our website. Please take the time to read this Privacy Policy carefully, as it contains essential details about how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data. By accessing our website, you confirm that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. Your continued use of our website is considered a freely given, specific, informed, and unambiguous consent to the processing of your personal data as outlined in this Policy.

Information We Collect:
We collect and process the following types of data:

  • Personal Information: When you contact us via our website, we may collect your name, email address, and any message content you submit.
  • Technical Information: When you visit our website, we collect anonymized data such as IP address, browser type, operating system, and usage details through cookies and analytics tools.
  • Communication Data: If you subscribe to our emails or contact us, we may store email exchanges.

    • How We Use Your Data:
    We use your personal data for the following purposes:
  • To respond to inquiries and manage communications.
  • To improve website functionality and enhance user experience.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.
  • For security purposes to prevent fraud and unauthorized access.
  • To analyse website traffic using Google Analytics.

    • Cookies and Tracking Technologies:
    Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience. Below is a breakdown of cookies used:
    Necessary Cookies (Essential for Website Functionality)
  • _GRECAPTCHA (Google reCAPTCHA): Used for spam prevention in our contact forms.
  • Local Storage: Stores user preferences for cookie consent.
  • fonts.gstatic.com & www.gstatic.com: Used to load Google Fonts and other essential Google services.
  • cdn.jsdelivr.net: Loads external libraries like Bootstrap and JavaScript dependencies.

    • Analytics & Performance Cookies (Require Consent)
  • Google Analytics (_ga, _gid, _gat): Tracks visitor activity, including page views and traffic sources.
  • region1.google-analytics.com: Part of Google Analytics, collects user behavior data.
  • www.googletagmanager.com: Manages tracking scripts, including Google Analytics.
  • www.google.com: May store cookies related to Google services.
    • These cookies are only enabled if the user consents to analytics tracking.

    Third-Party Services and EmailJS:
    We use EmailJS to handle contact form submissions securely. When you submit a message through our contact form, EmailJS processes the data (your name, email, and message) to deliver it to us. EmailJS does not store this data permanently. For more details, see EmailJS's Privacy Policy

    How We Store and Protect Your Data:
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • Data is retained only as long as necessary for the purposes stated in this policy.

    • Your Rights:
    Under GDPR and other data protection laws, you have the following rights:
  • The right to access, correct, or delete your data.
  • The right to withdraw consent for optional cookies at any time.
  • The right to request that we do not process your data for marketing purposes.
    • To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at: info@filmvaultgazette.com

    Managing Cookie Preferences:
    You can manage cookie preferences using our cookie consent banner or by adjusting your browser settings. Additionally, you can use the “Manage Cookies” button located at the bottom of our website to update your preferences at any time. Users can revoke consent for analytics cookies or opt out of non-essential tracking.

    Changes to This Policy:
    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be communicated via our website.
