Accessibility Policy for Film Vault Gazette

Introduction:
Film Vault Gazette is dedicated to curating monthly film image collections from Instagram users worldwide, with the aim of preserving this timeless art form for future generations. Our commitment extends to providing an inclusive online experience for all users.

Scope:
Our website features a combination of text and images contributed by artists. We ensure that the content is accessible, and proper credit is given to the artists, including tags of their Instagram handles. Film Vault Gazette is designed with a black and white color scheme, adhering to the WCAG 2.1 standards for both AA and AAA contrast, enhancing the accessibility of our platform.

Compliance Standards:
We adhere to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, meeting the standards for both AA and AAA contrast ratios. Our commitment to accessibility is an integral part of our mission to make the beauty of film images accessible to all.

User Feedback and Support:
Users are encouraged to report any accessibility issues or provide feedback via email, using the contact information provided in the Contact section. We are committed to responding to these reports promptly and implementing necessary improvements.

Future Improvements:
We have an ongoing plan for improvements to enhance accessibility on our website. Regular updates and enhancements are made to align with the latest developments in web accessibility.

Review and Revision:
Our accessibility policy is reviewed regularly to reflect changes in standards or practices. We are committed to evolving alongside technology to ensure a continually accessible user experience.

Film Vault Gazette values the diversity of its users and is dedicated to providing an inclusive platform for all film enthusiasts. If you have any accessibility concerns or suggestions, please feel free to reach out through the contact information provided in our Contact section.

Thank you for being a part of Film Vault Gazette.
