@_anna.onfilm

“I took this photo last year during my Erasmus in Berlin, when I lived right next to Gärten der Welt. The lighting was wonderful since it was a nice summer afternoon, and I simply liked the composition of the shot (and of course film always manages to bring out the best colours!). Simplicity and spontaneity are at the basis of my photography, and I always love to experiment with light, perspective and space.”
⭒
Ricoh Shotmaster Zoom Kodak ColorPlus 200
Gärten der Welt

@projectartz12

“Having been in film photography for over a year now, the experience of shooting in film and experimenting with light, shadows and colours has given me joy. The organic colours and the soft texture from the film output brings about retro moments.

This film features the colourful houses on the cliff in Positano. Shooting this on Kodak Portra 400 gives it a fine grain look and a nice blend of pastel colours which reflects the vintage/retro atmosphere of the place itself.”
⭒
Rolleiflex 3.5F Kodak Portra 400
Positano, Amalfi Coast, Italy

Sarah (filmasigo)

“While shooting film I crave the authenticity of one shot, capturing the magic in a single frame. In Chefchaouen, I was mesmerized by the vibrant blue hues and the way the setting sun bathed everything in a warm, golden light. This moment, where beauty and culture converge, felt perfectly aligned, and I knew I had to preserve it.”
⭒
Olympus OM-10 Kodak Portra 160
