@35mm.iv

“The 1980s camera given to me by my godfather is a frequent travel companion. This photo was taken in Sicily, a land of lemons and vespas. In particular, this chandelier is located in the oldest royal residence in Europe, the Royal Palace of Palermo. If we get a bit philosophical, the camera can be seen as a time blender, connecting centuries-old creations with a simple click of a button on the top right corner of this tiny device.”
⭒
Canon T80 Kodak Gold 200
Palazzo Normanni, Palermo, Sicily

Pentax P30 Kodak Gold 200
