@oldcarsonfilm

“My name is Christian. I am 54 and still regularly use my AT-1 I bought at 19. However, I have a different approach now wanting to test every format and every era. Also, now I develop b&w and C-41 and have just bought chemistry for slide. That very photo was my first test of a fisheye at the Mercedes museum. I like testing and I often carry half a dozen cameras and I wanted to compare HP5+ and Delta 3200 both at 1600. Unfortunately I am sometimes absentminded and I left the Diana on bulb mode after pinhole tests a month ago. Thus many photos are blurry but I discovered it before ending that roll I was using, thus the Porsche is not blurry.”
