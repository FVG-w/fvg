Christina Bourbon (@cold_pencil_)

“This photo is a double exposure of the same view of Mount Rainer with the camera flipped the other way for the second exposure. I try to use double exposures a lot in my photography in hopes that it makes people reimagine the world around them. It also forces me to trust the process since I truly have no idea how the image will turn out.“
