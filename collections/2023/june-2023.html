Jan-Erik Hoel (@the_objective_camera)

"This particular shot was taken on my way from work. There was a storm building up, and I happened to have my Canon in the car. I stopped and snapped a few, because the lighting was absolutely stunning. I use Lightroom a lot to get my photos gloomy. I'm not ashamed to admit. The grainyness comes for free by shooting with the 35mm gold 200 film with minimal light exposure. I love the moody, grainy look it creates - and with small light adjustments the result can be incredible!
This is basically how create this style to all my cinematic/ moody shots - but I like to experiment and play. Im very new in this massive universe of filmphotography, so I tend to try out different methods and styles, just to see what comes out of it. Once you get to know your camera well, composition is everything. I usually never plan my objects/motives. I shoot whatever I find interesting."
⭒
Canon F-1 Kodak Gold 200
Moss, Norway
