February 2025 Collection

Leo (@grain.tiff)

“About my photography I`d say that I take pictures of anything I like and enjoy and over time the list of such things grows as I continue to learn about myself and world around me. I like shooting on film as I feel it keeps me more intended with the shots and I`m grateful for the people I`ve met and interactions with strangers because of my film cameras.”
⭒
Nikon FM Lomo 800

Yousef Almehdari (@gyousefandyousef)

“On a morning walk exploring the old ship gravyard in the shallow waters of Doha port in Kuwait, I found an abandoned ship stands rusting in shallow waters - a silent reminder of journeys long past and a future left behind.

I like finding poetry in the everyday scenes and capturing beauty in the mundane, one frame at a time. I shoot in both color & B/W, prioritizing mood over perfection.”
⭒
Leica M6 Fomapan 400
Qatar - Doha

@smalldropofgrace

“This photo was taken of a close friend of mine while she performed her new music for me. She nor the wind could stand still. In all my work, I aim to express the intimacy in human connection to the world around them.”
⭒
Nikon FM2 Kodak Gold 200

Valerio Vaccaro (@il_vak)

“I am not a professional photographer but simply an amateur. In my photos I try to represent the reality that surrounds me, interfering with it as little as possible. My main goal is to convey a sense of nostalgic serenity with my photos.”
⭒
Pentax LX Kodak Vision3 50D
50mm Pentax f1.4 Sicily, Italy

Lesley (@newspaper.people)

“This double exposure was taken while roadtripping down California`s Pacific Coast Highway. I wanted to capture the feeling of when you make your way to a destination where what you see on the map becomes a tangible place.”
⭒
Canon EOS Rebel G Kodak Gold 200
Pacific Coast Highway

Yannick Martineau (@tinofaitdelaphoto)

“A gentleman who trusts me has given me access to his private collection of old, run-down cars in his backyard. On some Saturdays, I go there, lose myself in photography, and forget everything else. It`s something that truly brings me peace.

This photo is one of about 350 I`ve taken so far, and I fully intend to keep going back!”
⭒
Nikon F3 Kodak Gold 200
