About Us

We believe that film photography is not just a hobby or a trend, but a valuable legacy that should be treasured and shared.

As we grow, we hope to create a virtual doomsday vault of film photography as well as a vibrant and diverse community with opportunities for film photographers to showcase their work and connect to like-minded individuals.

Join our community of film lovers and discover the beauty of analog photography. Submit your film images by tagging our Instagram account @filmvaultgazette or using #filmvaultgazette for a chance to be featured on our page and added to the collection.
